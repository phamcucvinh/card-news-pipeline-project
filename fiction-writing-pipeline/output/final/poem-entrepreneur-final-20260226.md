# First Draft
*페르소나: 창업 실패자 (Failed Entrepreneur) — Remember 스타일*
*작성일: 2026.02.26*

---

What collapsed was not you —
it was the first draft.
Do not confuse the two.

Every person who built something real
has a room full of things that did not work.
They simply kept the door closed.

The market moves. The idea dies.
But the one who learned
was never the idea.

There is no failed life —
only unfinished ones.

---

**형식**: 자유시 (Free verse) — Remember 스타일
**행수**: 12행
**핵심 이미지**: 무너진 것 = 초고, 무너지지 않은 것 = 사람
**작가 노트**: 실패와 실패자를 분리하는 것이 핵심. 마지막 행은 미완성의 긍정적 재정의.
