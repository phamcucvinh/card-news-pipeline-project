# Still Yours
*페르소나: 실연한 사람 (Heartbroken) — Remember 스타일*
*작성일: 2026.02.26*

---

They were not taken from you.
They left when it was time.
Do not chase what has already gone.

What you loved in them
still lives inside you —
that tenderness is yours to keep.

The ache you carry now
will one day become the thing
that makes you gentle with another.

Love is not a name.
It is something you learn to do again.

---

**형식**: 자유시 (Free verse) — Remember 스타일
**행수**: 12행
**핵심 이미지**: 사라진 사람 → 남겨진 온기
**작가 노트**: 이별은 상실이 아닌 재편성이다. 사랑했던 감정은 없어지지 않고 다른 형태로 이어진다.
