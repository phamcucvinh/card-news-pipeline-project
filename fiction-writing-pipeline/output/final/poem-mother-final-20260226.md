# What I Leave You
*페르소나: 어머니가 자식에게 (Mother) — Remember 스타일*
*작성일: 2026.02.26*

---

You will fall.
That is not failure —
that is how you learn where the ground is.

There will be people who leave
before you are ready.
Let them go. The ones who stay will be enough.

Do not rush toward the life you imagined.
The one that finds you
will fit better than anything you planned.

I cannot always be there.
But what I gave you — that stays.

---

**형식**: 자유시 (Free verse) — Remember 스타일
**행수**: 12행
**핵심 이미지**: 넘어짐 → 바닥의 위치를 배움
**작가 노트**: 어머니의 목소리로 쓰되 설교가 아닌 진실을 건넨다. 마지막 행은 부재 속 존재의 역설.
