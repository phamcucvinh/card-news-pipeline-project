# What Was Real
*페르소나: 노인 (Elder) — Remember 스타일*
*작성일: 2026.02.26*

---

The years you worried about
are already behind you.
You survived what you feared.

Most of what you held so tightly —
the opinions, the small wars —
none of it was as large as it felt.

The people who mattered
were there longer than you noticed.
You only see it now.

Time is not a thief.
It is the only thing that tells you what was real.

---

**형식**: 자유시 (Free verse) — Remember 스타일
**행수**: 12행
**핵심 이미지**: 붙잡았던 것들의 실제 크기
**작가 노트**: 시간이 지나야만 보이는 것들에 대한 조용한 회고. 시간을 도둑이 아닌 판별자로 재정의한다.
