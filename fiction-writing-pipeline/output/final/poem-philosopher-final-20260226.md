# What You Know
*페르소나: 철학자 (Philosopher) — Remember 스타일*
*작성일: 2026.02.26*

---

What you know today
will be undone by tomorrow.
Do not cling to certainty.

The mind that admits it does not know
is already closer to the answer
than the one that stopped asking.

Even the truest thought, held too long,
becomes a cage.
Let go of what you once believed.

There is no final wisdom.
There is only the one who keeps walking.

---
